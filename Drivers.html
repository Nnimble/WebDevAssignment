Meet the Rookies

Oscar Piastri

Driving for McLaren with 4 points

Oscar Piastri is a 20-year-old Australian racing driver who currently competes in the Formula One World Championship for McLaren. He was born on December 6, 2001, in Melbourne, Australia, and began his racing career in karting before moving up to single-seaters. Piastri won the 2020 FIA Formula 3 Championship and was signed by McLaren as a development driver for the 2021 season. In 2022, he was promoted to a full-time race seat alongside Lando Norris. Piastri is regarded as one of the most promising young drivers in motorsport and is expected to have a bright future in Formula One.

Logan Sargeant

Driving for Williams with 0 points

Logan Sargeant is an American racing driver who currently competes for the Williams team in Formula One. Born on November 31, 2000, in Florida, Sargeant began his racing career in karting and eventually moved on to single-seater racing. He has competed in various junior categories, including F4, F3, and Formula Renault Eurocup, and achieved multiple podium finishes and victories.Sargeant is considered a promising young talent in the sport and has already shown his potential with strong performances in the junior categories.

Nyck De Vries is a Dutch racing driver who currently competes in Formula One for the AlphaTauri team in the 2023 season. Born on 6 February 1995 in Sneek, Netherlands, De Vries began his racing career in karting before moving up to various single-seater categories. He won the 2019 FIA Formula 2 Championship and subsequently joined the Mercedes Formula E team. In 2023, he made his debut in Formula One with the AlphaTauri team.
